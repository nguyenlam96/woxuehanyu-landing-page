Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Wo xue Hanyu ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Chinese language learning app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information (optional): Email address, name, profile picture
  • Learning Data: Progress, completed lessons, quiz results, study streaks
  • User Preferences: App settings, notification preferences, language preferences
  • Communication: Support requests, feedback, survey responses

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent, lesson completion rates
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Technical Data: App version, crash reports, performance metrics
  • Analytics: Aggregated usage patterns to improve the app

1.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect payment information (handled by Apple and Google)
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide Services

  • Deliver personalized learning content
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Sync data across your devices
  • Provide customer support

2.2 To Improve Our App

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance
  • Develop new content and features

2.3 To Communicate

  • Send important app updates
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send learning reminders (if you opt-in)

2.4 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and prevent misuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Use

3.1 Where We Store Data

  • Primary servers: Supabase (hosted on AWS)
  • Data centers located in: [Specify regions, e.g., US, EU]
  • Backup systems: Encrypted cloud storage

3.2 How We Protect Data

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Strict authentication and authorization

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your learning data
  • Object: Opt-out of certain data processing

5.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

  • In-App: Go to Settings > Account > Privacy Settings
  • Email: Contact woxuehanyu.mobile@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We respond within 30 days

5.3 Manage Communications

  • Push Notifications: Turn off in device settings or app settings
  • Emails: Unsubscribe link in all emails

Parents: If you have any questions or concerns about your child's use of the app, please contact us at woxuehanyu.mobile@gmail.com.

9. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. For a dedicated deletion guide, visit https://woxuehanyu.site/delete-account.

Steps:

  1. Open Wo xue Hanyu app
  2. Go to Settings > Account
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Alternative request method: Email woxuehanyu.mobile@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered email address.

What Gets Deleted:

  • Personal information (name, email)
  • Boya and HSK learning progress and history
  • Saved vocabulary and bookmarks
  • User preferences and settings
  • All associated data

What May Be Retained:

  • Transaction, invoice, and subscription records required for legal, tax, accounting, or payment-provider compliance
  • Limited technical or security logs required to prevent fraud or respond to lawful requests

What Happens:

  • Immediate: Account deactivated
  • Within 30 days: All data permanently deleted
  • Within 90 days: Removed from all backups

Note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Active subscriptions must be cancelled separately through Apple.
